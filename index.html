OVERVIEW

Garena is a leading advocate and organizer of Esports events in Greater Southeast Asia, which strengthens our game ecosystem and increases user engagement.Garena is the exclusive operator of top-tier games in Greater Southeast Asia. Through our Garena platform, users can access popular and engaging mobile and PC online games.Users can connect via Garena Platform to connect with their fellow gamers, get the latest news and updates around the gaming community.

ABOUT US

Player Stats: Provide a platform where players can view their in-game statistics, such as kills, wins, playtime, and rank progression. Leaderboards: Implement leaderboards to showcase top players based on various metrics like kills, wins, and overall ranking. This can create a competitive environment and encourage players to improve their skills. Guides and Tips: Offer comprehensive guides, tips, and strategies to help players enhance their gameplay, master different weapons, and improve their overall performance in Free Fire.
